School Facilities

Information & Health Facilities

Computer Lab

A modern Computer Lab equipped with desktop computers and networking peripherals. This facility is used for teaching computer studies, conducting e-learning sessions, and preparing students for Computer-Based Tests (CBT) such as the JAMB and BECE.

IBB Library

IBB Library is a quiet study environment containing a collection of textbooks, reference materials, and literature. It serves as the academic hub of the college, independent study, and the development of literacy skills across all grade levels.

School Clinic

A healthcare unit staffed by trained medical personnel to provide first aid and primary health services to students and staff. It manages minor ailments and emergencies, ensuring the physical well-being of the boarding community.

Science Laboratories

Biology Lab

A specialized space equipped with biological specimens, microscopes, and anatomical charts. It is designed for the study of living organisms, plant physiology, and dissection exercises required for the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).

Chemistry Lab

A functional laboratory featuring workbenches with sinks and drainage systems. it is stocked with essential reagents, glassware (beakers, flasks, burettes), and safety equipment for conducting chemical analysis, titrations, and qualitative experiments.

Physics Lab

A dedicated facility housing apparatus for mechanics, optics, electricity, and magnetism. It provides students with the tools necessary to verify physical laws through experiments involving circuits, lenses, and pendulums.

Government College Bida
Established 1912

Government College, Bida, established in 1912, evolving through names like Bida Middle School and Niger Provincial Secondary School before becoming Government College, Bida in 1970.
