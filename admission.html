Admission Procedures

To gain admission into Government College Bida (GCB), a state-owned secondary school in Niger State, below are the standardized process managed by the Niger State Ministry of Basic and Secondary Education Board that candidate must follow.

Admission into Junior Secondary School (JSS 1)

Admission at this level is strictly for pupils completing their primary education.

  • Eligibility: Candidates must typically be at least 12 years old by September 1st of the enrolment year.
  • Request free Admission form from School: Applicants Request Admission form from Government College Bida, which will be submitted by school to State Ministry of Education.
  • Placement: Based on the form submitted, successful candidates are placed into Government College Bida. There is usually no admission for JSS 2 or JSS 3.

Admission into Senior Secondary School (SS 1)

Admission into the senior section is for students transitioning from Junior Secondary School.

  • BECE Requirement: Students must pass the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) conducted by the National Examinations Council (NECO) or the State Ministry of Education.
  • Request free Admission form from School: Applicants Request Admission form from Government College Bida, which will be submitted by school to State Ministry of Education.
  • Placement List: The Ministry releases an official admission list (often available via the Niger State Ministry of Education portal).
  • Documentation & Clearance: Once admitted, students must report to the school for documentation with the following:
    1. Original Admission Letter printed from the ministry's portal.
    2. BECE Statement of Result (for SS 1 only).
    3. Birth Certificate or official declaration of age.
    4. Certificate of Indigene from their Local Government Area.
    5. Medical Certificate of Fitness from a government-approved hospital.

Key Rules and Levies

  • Fees: While tuition is generally covered by the state, students are required to pay a PTA Levy to the school.
  • Uniforms: Parents are expected to provide uniforms and sportswear based on samples displayed at the school.
  • Prohibited Items: Electronics, mobile phones (GSM), and dangerous weapons are strictly prohibited on the school premises.
Government College Bida
Established 1912

Government College, Bida, established in 1912, evolving through names like Bida Middle School and Niger Provincial Secondary School before becoming Government College, Bida in 1970.
